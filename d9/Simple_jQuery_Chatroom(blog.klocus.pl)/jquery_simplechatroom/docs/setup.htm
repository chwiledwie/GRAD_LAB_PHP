Setup

Upload the contents of the 'www' folder to your server, to whichever subfolder you wish.

Place a link from your main page (or any page you want to) on your website to the "chatroom.php" file wherever you uploaded it on your server (for example: http://www.your-domain.com/chat/chatroom.php). Check the link is working, then your chatroom is ready!
